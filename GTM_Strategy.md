## 3. Monetization Strategy (Business Architecture)

### Phase 1: Aggressive User Acquisition (Free)
*   **Model:** 100% Free with "Viral Loops".
*   **Goal:** Build a massive user base to train our AI and populate leaderboards.
*   **Mechanism:** Infinite free challenges, but "Invite 3 friends to unlock [Custom Theme/Icon]".

### Phase 2: Freemium (B2C)
*   **SmartSip PRO ($2.99/mo):**
    *   Advanced AI Health Correlation (e.g., "You drink less on Tuesdays, here's why...").
    *   Unlimited Challenge History.
    *   Exclusive "Glass Mode" & "Zen Garden" Themes.

### Phase 3: Corporate Plans (B2B)
*   **SmartSip for Teams ($49/mo per team):**
    *   Company-wide Leaderboards.
    *   Automated Slack/Teams Integrations.
    *   Impact Reports (e.g., "Your team drank 500L this month!").
